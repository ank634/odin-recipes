Tacos

Description

Burritos are Tacos and extremely versatile food to make. Today I will show you how you can Make a Tacos with just about any ingredients in your kitchen.

ingredients

  • A corn Tortilla
  • Flank Steak
  • Oranges
  • Sliced onion

Steps

  1. Squeeze juice from oranges into a bowl and put flank steak, onion, and ornage peels in bowl. Let marinade for 3 hrs in fridge
  2. After marinating is complete put steak on skillet for about 7 minutes
  3. Afer steak is cooked slice into small pieces
  4. Put steak into tortilla and enjoy
