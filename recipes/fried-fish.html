Fried Fish

Description

Fried Fish are delious and extremely versatile food to make. Today I will show you how you can Make Fried Fish with a side of pico de gallo

ingredients

  • Tilapia
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh Roma Tomatos
  • White Onion

Steps

  1. Season your fish with salt and pepper
  2. Apply oil to Pan
  3. Let the fish cook for about 10 minutes
  4. Dice tomates and onions at a 2 to 1 ratio
  5. Mix tomatoes and onion in a bowl
  6. Pour Pico de Gallo over Fish!
