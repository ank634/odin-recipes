Burrito

Description

Burritos are delious and extremely versatile food to make. Today I will show you how you can Make a burrito with just about any ingredients in your kitchen.

ingredients

  • A Flour Tortilla
  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Any Protein

Steps

  1. Lay your Tortilla down flat and add a think layer of beans
  2. Add a thin layer of rice on top of the thin layer of Rice
  3. Next chop your protein into small bite size pieces
  4. Liberally add your protein to the top of the Rice
  5. Wrap your burrito up and you are all done!
